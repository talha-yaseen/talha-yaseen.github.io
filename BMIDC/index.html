About BMIDC

The Beaconhouse Diplomats' Conference is a premier debating competition that focuses on discussing and resolving issues in different circumstances under the agenda of nine well established committees. The Conference is a three day simulation where delegates take the role of diplomats and nations alike, all the while striving to solve international issues and promote welfare and peace. Finally, for entertainment, we offer participants one of a kind social events that will radiate an air of exuberance.

Unlike an ordinary MUN, BMIDC aims at combining intellect with innovation and bring out quality like you've never seen before. Offering an immersive experience, BMIDC boasts intriguing and interesting crisis committees that require quick and well enhanced thinking. Capturing your imagination and inspiring innovation, we promise to produce an event of a lifetime.
