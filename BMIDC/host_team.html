Executive Council

Secretary General

Afnan Arshad

Assalam-o-Alaikum!

BMIDC is back, better than ever, here to give you 3 memorable days of enthusiastic debate mingled with a blend of social events. Continuing its legacy, BMIDC will provide the platform for all kinds of debaters to come and match their skills against some of the very best speakers from all across Pakistan. Never-before-seen committees like the 'Supreme Court' along with an awe-inspiring secretariat are sure to provide you with an unmatched debating experience. Unlike most MUNs, BMIDC shall be simulating all its committees in a unique way, a combination of crisis and conventional procedure, promising the delegates the time they spend here will be unforgettable.

Complementing these ardent committee sessions will be an unprecedented array of social events that will undoubtedly complete your BMIDC experience and satisfy your inner debater.

Hope to see you all there!
