UNHRC

United Nations Human Rights Council

The Human Rights council was introduced into the United Nations to oversee the protection and promotion of human rights around the globe. This committee aims to create awareness amongst delegates about the increasing problems concerning human rights, and reaches solutions to protect the welfare of the common people, and has allowed countries with poor human rights records to be an active part of it. The delegates will discuss some of the most pressing issues faced by the world today, and prepare for it accordingly. This committee aims to discuss all active clashes in the world as well as other critical situations concerning human rights so as to give opportunities to debaters to look at these situations from every angle, and help solve the problems.
