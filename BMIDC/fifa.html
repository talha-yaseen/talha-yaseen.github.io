FIFA

Federation Internationale de Football Association

The Federation Internationale de Football Association is the international governing body of Football, Futsal and Beach Soccer. It is governed by the Swiss Law founded in 1904 and is based in Zurich. With 209 member associations, FIFA constantly aims at fulfilling its goal to work tirelessly at improving the standard of football around the globe.

Its administration is carried out by the General Secretariat which employs about 400 members and deals with divisions that include development, competitions, football administration, finance, business, personnel, services and communications.
