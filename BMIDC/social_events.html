Cultural Fest

What better way to kick things off than the ever so popular global village, right? Indeed-However this year we redefine its purpose and bring you the all new cultural fest which while keeping the original tradition alive; amalgamating the real feeling of global festivities all around you. Blending unique styles from across the world, we'll throw you (literally) under the spotlight. Represent your country's ravishing culture, delicious cuisine and remarkable lineage as you dwell into an atmosphere that'll surely leave everyone awe-struck.

Concert & Electronight

The stress of the conference taking its toll on you? All those arguments hurting your head?
