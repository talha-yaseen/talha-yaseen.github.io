Capture It!

"In photography there is a reality so subtle that it becomes more real than reality."

Category Head

Azam Shah

Team Cap

  • A delegation may send one team only.
  • The team may consist of 1-2 people.

Procedure

  • It will consist of 3 rounds.
  • 1st round about nature.
  • 2nd round about Art.
  • 3rd round about capturing candid pictures.

Rules

  • Fully manual photography
  • Good editing skills
  • Tripod not allowed

Judging

  • Based on vivid colours
  • Idea and story behind the picture

Awards

  • Best team: 6 points
  • Runners-up: 4 points
