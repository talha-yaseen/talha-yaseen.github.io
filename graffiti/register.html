Registrations Process

  • Each institution may send one team in their respective delegation and the maximum number of participants allowed in a team are 25.
  • The minimum number of participants allowed, for a delegation to be registered, is 10 delegates.
  • Each delegate will be required to participate in, at least one category.
  • No team will be accommodated after the registrations deadline which shall be posted on the official website in due time before the event.
  • All payments should be made in accordance with the deadlines posted on the website or the Facebook page.
  • Kindly make the cheque payable to "Lahore Grammar School Pvt. Limited"
  • Any changes in teams should be communicated to Lahore Grammar School Islamabad at least a week prior to the event.
  • Incomplete registration forms i.e. incomplete fields/missing pictures/signatures etc. will not be reviewed.
  • Each school must the online registration forms, the links of which are available below.
  • Registration forms and payments must be received before a Delegation can be registered.
  • Lahore Grammar School Islamabad reserves the right to decline any application.

Code of Conduct

Delegates must refrain from the following:

  • Physical violence, use of foul language and misbehaving with other participants or the management.
  • Damaging the school property or entering any restricted area.
  • Using unfair means to gain an advantage over the other participants.
  • Arguing with the judges over the results; the results and the judge's decisions are final and cannot be questioned.
  • Smoking, use of alcohol or any other intoxicant on campus.
  • Creating excessive noise or disturbance on campus.
  • Portraying of violence, profanity, drug abuse and any other offensive material.
  • Misusing or damaging any of the equipment provided by the management.
  • Dressing in inappropriate attire at any time of the event.
  • The Host Team will not be responsible for any misplaced belongings.
